A Playground for Creatives

Welcome to Design Box

Inspiration by design

Part of the Innovasia Group, DESIGNBOX offers a worldwide network of interior fabrics, leather, wallcovering, and furniture for hospitality, commercial and residential projects.

In our showroom in Escazu, Costa Rica, designers are welcome to use our resource library to explore design possibilities. We carefully curate internationally recognized brands across the design and pricing spectrum to suit any project.

Our Services

We specialize in representing a wide range of materials for interior design projects. At Design Box, creative professionals such as architects, interior decorators, designers, etc., can find the best material brands. In our showroom located in Escazú, our team can advise you and help you create a material library for your project. For a more personalized visit, you can schedule an appointment with us.

Meet the Team

Innovasia, INC.
